[UncertaiNLP](https://uncertainlp.github.io)'s 3rd edition will be held at EMNLP 2026 in Budapest. The workshop brings together researchers studying uncertainty in human language and NLP tools, including LLMs, and how it can be harnessed to improve NLP. Don't miss the CFP (deadlines: August 7, 2026 for direct submissions).
